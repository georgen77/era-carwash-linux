🍽️ Food & Drinks — Must Try

Authentic Paella
Valencia invented paella — eat it for lunch (never dinner!). Best: La Pepica on the beachfront or Casa Carmela.
🥤
Horchata & Fartons
Valencia's iconic tiger-nut drink, ice-cold with fluffy pastry sticks. Try Horchatería de Santa Catalina.

Fresh orange juice + cava + vodka + gin. Order it by the pitcher on a terrace at sunset!
